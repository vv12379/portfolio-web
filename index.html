Boom-Bap Beat Production

Original boom-bap beat at 90 BPM in the key of Dm, built from synthesized drum samples. Programmed punchy kick, snare, and hi-hat patterns on the step sequencer, then layered a sub-bass line and Dm chord pad using the piano roll. Includes a minor pentatonic melody that enters on bar 3 with swing-timed hi-hats. Final mix with EQ shaping and limiting.

About

Background

Audio and video production specialist with hands-on experience in open-source digital media tools. I focus on clean audio processing, creative sound design, and polished video post-production for digital content and multimedia workflows.

My work spans voice cleanup and enhancement using Audacity, multitrack mixing and mastering in Ardour, beat production and MIDI programming in LMMS, and video editing with color grading in Shotcut and OpenShot.

I bring strong attention to detail in signal processing, a systematic approach to project workflows, and adaptability across different production environments and output formats.
